All pairs below looked suspicious but were confirmed as intended.

  alt shows a dark-haired female warrior in colorful foliage.
  Both are warriors.
- [x] **Breaker the Magical Warrior** (5655) → 22081: original shows a red/gold armored warrior with staff;
  alt shows the same figure in an impressionistic style, with the dragon likely in the background.
- [x] **Junk Warrior** (7696) → 19077: original is a purple mechanical warrior;
  alt shows a silver/white armored warrior with wings in purple energy.

  alt shows a white cloaked figure with crystal/diamond effects. Styles are very different but confirmed.

  The alt art depicts the most common Tuner target of the card effect.

  alt shows two Ice Barrier children characters.
